CALLEBAUT SIGNATURE COLLECTION - MILK ORIGIN CHOCOLATE - SILKY GHANA - 2.5KG - CALLETS

Gentle sweetness unfolds into rich chestnut warmth with bright plum freshness, delivering smooth, luxurious texture and seamless balance.

very high fluidity

40% Min. % Dry cocoa solids
20% Min. % Dry milk solids
40% Fat %

The forest regions of Ghana and its time-honoured cultivation practices come together beautifully in Silky Experience Ghana 40%, where generations of families have been running farms to create exceptional milk chocolate. This distinguished milk chocolate from our Signature Collection pays homage to Ghana's status as the world's second-largest cocoa producer. Traditional farming methods and optimal growing conditions consistently produce beans renowned for their clean, balanced flavour. The meticulous selection and processing of the beans showcase the qualities that have established Ghanaian cocoa as the global benchmark for excellence. Silky Ghana 40% offers a smooth, comforting, and indulgent flavour journey. It opens with a dominant sweet taste and a rich milky flavour, immediately creating a soft and welcoming first impression. As the tasting unfolds, milky and roasted cocoa notes emerge, beautifully balanced by the ongoing sweetness. In the second half, the profile deepens with caramel and roasted cocoa flavours, adding a warm and creamy richness. The experience finishes with a lingering sweet taste, leaving a lasting impression of comfort and indulgence. This 40% cocoa couverture is ideal for professional applications where performance and flavour excellence are paramount. Its superior flow properties and silky texture make it ideal for precise moulding, elegant enrobing and creating sophisticated ganaches. Its balanced composition ensures consistent results across a range of applications, from delicate confectionery to complex dessert components. Silky Ghana 40% provides discerning professionals with the reliability and quality they expect.
